How to Work Legally in 3 Steps

1. Register Online

Apply through the e-Work Permit portal with your employer’s details.

2. Medical Check

Visit a certified hospital for health verification and biometrics.

3. Stamp & Smart Card

Receive your digital permit and physical smart card at the labor office.

Know Your Rights!

  • Your employer CANNOT keep your passport.
  • You are entitled to the same minimum wage as Thai citizens.
  • You have the right to medical treatment under Social Security.
  • You can report unfair treatment to the Labor Hotline 1546.
